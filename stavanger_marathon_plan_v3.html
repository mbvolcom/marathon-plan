Sub-threshold feel: You could speak in short sentences but wouldn't want to. Comfortably hard — well below gasping. This is the bread-and-butter session throughout the plan.

Near-threshold (weeks 8–13): A notch harder. You can manage a word or two but it requires effort. Be honest — if you're on or over threshold, back off. The stimulus is in the volume, not the intensity.

MP segments on long runs: These replace one of the weekly sub-threshold sessions as the plan progresses. Run these as a block in the middle or end of the long run — not the whole thing.

Strides: 20-second smooth accelerations at the end of easy runs. Not sprints — controlled and relaxed.
